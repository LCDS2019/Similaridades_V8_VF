All patients present with ectodermal dysplasia that is characterised by hypocalcified amelogenesis imperfecta and leads to the loss of soft dental enamel. In addition, patients present at birth with congenital myopathy, which is characterised by non-progressive generalised muscular dysplasia. Caused by mutations in the ORAI1 and STIM1 genes (12q24 and 11p15.5). Transmission is autosomal recessive." @en-gb
