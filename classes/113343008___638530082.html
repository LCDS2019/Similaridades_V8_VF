Class: 'Body organ structure (body structure)'

Annotations (4)

  • rdfs:label "Body organ structure (body structure)" @en
  • skos:altLabel "Body organ" @en
  • skos:definition "An anatomical structure that consists of the maximal set of organ parts so connected to one another that together they constitute a self-contained unit of macroscopic anatomy, distinct both morphologically and functionally from other such units. Together with other organs, an organ constitutes an organ system or a body part. An organ is divisible into organ parts but not organs (examples: femur, biceps, liver, heart, aorta, sciatic nerve, ovary)." @en
