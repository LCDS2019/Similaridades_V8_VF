Annotations (3)

  • rdfs:label "Allergic reaction (disorder)" @en
  • skos:definition "A pathological immune process generally directed towards a foreign antigen, which results in tissue injury, which is usually transient. It is the realization of the allergic disposition. It is most often applied to type I hypersensitivity but other hypersensitivity types especially type IV (e.g. allergic contact dermatitis) may be involved." @en
