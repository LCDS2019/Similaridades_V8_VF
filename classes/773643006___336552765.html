Craniofacial dysmorphism (prominent occiput, enlarged fontanelle, fused metopic suture, upslanted palpebral fissures, over folded helix, depressed nasal bridge, anteverted nose, malar flattening, Pierre-Robin sequence, high arched palate, short neck) and other manifestations (joint contractures, hyperreflexia, dysplastic nails, developmental delay) are also observed. Caused by mutation in the PIGA gene on chromosome Xp22." @en
